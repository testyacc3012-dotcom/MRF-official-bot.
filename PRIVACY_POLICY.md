# Privacy Policy — MRF Official Bot

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This policy explains what information this bot collects, why, and how it's used.

## What Data Is Collected

When moderators use this bot's commands, the following information may be recorded:

- **Discord User IDs** of the person being moderated and the moderator issuing the action.
- **Discord usernames/tags**, for display in logs and warning records.
- **Warning reasons and timestamps**, when a `/warn` is issued, stored so staff can review a user's history with `/warnings`.
- **Moderation action records** (bans, kicks, timeouts, etc.), which may be posted to a server's designated log channel if one is configured.

This bot does **not** collect message content beyond what's needed for the `/purge` command to function in real time (it is not stored afterward), and does not track members' activity outside of the moderation commands staff choose to run.

## How Data Is Used

Collected data is used solely to:
- Provide moderation history (warnings) within the server where the action occurred.
- Send the affected user a direct message notifying them of the action, the reason, and an appeal link.
- Optionally post a record of the action to that server's log channel.

## Data Storage

Warning records are stored per-server and per-user. Data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties, and is not used for advertising.

## Data Retention & Deletion

Warning data persists until a moderator clears it using `/clearwarnings`, or until the bot's hosting is reset/redeployed. If you would like data related to your account removed, contact us via the support server linked below.

## Third-Party Services

This bot is hosted using third-party infrastructure (such as Railway) for running the bot process. Discord's own Privacy Policy (https://discord.com/privacy) governs data handled by Discord itself, separate from what this bot stores.

## Children's Privacy

This bot operates within Discord, which requires users to meet Discord's own minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect data from users who do not meet those requirements.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact

For questions about this policy or to request data removal, join the support server: https://discord.gg/8nPqvmJC2r
